Introduction

Hi New York District Key Club,

Each year, during the New York District Key Club’s District Convention, the Leadership Training Conference, we present the Distinguished Key Clubber Award to our most dedicated members, who have gone above and beyond the call of duty, shown leadership throughout the service year, and promoted the core values of Key Club International. Members who have served their homes, schools and communities to the best of their abilities and consistently worked throughout the service year are eligible to receive this award. All members who would like to receive this award must complete this application.

This year’s application has been redesigned and remodeled to reflect the high ideals of Key Club International. It has been made easier and more flexible so that more members may receive the award. Anyone who does not fulfill all the requirements and are in a club with special circumstances may still submit the application.

We also worked with our district webmaster, Danny Qiu, to create a new method for electronic submissions. Please create an account at http://dkc-app.nydkc.org and follow the online instructions. We will continue to accept submissions via snail mail. For verification, please follow the online directions provided on the website, or have the appropriate persons sign the verification statement for snail mail submissions.

If any information provided in this application is inaccurate, dishonest, unverified, or warranted void, your application may be disqualified. Thank you for your service to your home, school and community.

We wish you the best of luck in your application and anticipate your submission.

Yours in Care and in Service,

Lieutenant Governor Aaron Cheung
District Awards Committee Co-Chair

Lieutenant Governor Cindy Pietrakowski
District Awards Committee Co-Chair

Instructions & Rules

  1. The application is separated into four parts, and each part is designed to focus on different aspects of Key Club. Complete all parts of this application to qualify for the award.
    • Part 1: Personal Statement
    • Part 2: Mandatory Projects
    • Part 3: Involvement
    • Part 4: Projects, Advocacy & Newsletter
  2. If you are submitting the application by mail, please obtain the signatures of all the parties in the Verification section of the PDF application. Applications without verification WILL BE DISQUALIFIED. If you are submitting this application electronically, please complete your verification in the Verification section.
  3. All applications should be submitted by the deadline. All late applications will be penalized at 5 points from each section per day. Any applications more than 3 days late will not be accepted.
  4. If you are having technical difficulties, you must contact your Committee Co-Chair by 8:00 PM EST on the night the application is due. If you have technical difficulties that are not reported by 8:00 PM EST, it will not be honored.
  5. By submitting this application, the applicant agrees that all information included is accurate and that the information provided is his/her own work. Please describe events in your own words. Any form of plagiarism will DISQUALIFY the application. Any form of dishonesty will DISQUALIFY the application.
  6. All applications are subject to be disqualified as deemed fit by a Committee Co-Chair.

Notes:

  • For all events, please include the number of service hours, the cause or charity that was helped, the date and location of the event, and a short description.
  • Read the directions for each section CAREFULLY. If you do not provide all information, you will not be given credit.
